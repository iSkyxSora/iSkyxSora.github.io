My First PC Build

This was my frist PC I have ever build. The tower, video card, and the power supply were old parts from my friend. As you can see, I actually repainted the tower white. The reason why I did that was because it would match my motherboard better and I thought it looks much nicer when it is white. LOL!

Working this tower was a huge pain. One because the case's design were old, so it is much harder to build on it compare to the newer towers nowadays. Two, we forgot to remove some screws inside the tower before spraying, so the screw for the motherboard took forever to get on because of the paint. somehow everything manage to workout. The powersupply was also modular, which is why it looks extremely messy in the tower. I tried my best to clean up the wires and I think I did a fairly decent job. I don't know if you can tell, but I actually used a piece of cardboard to hold up my SSD (very getto), in those image, but I recently did get a SSD holder.

Wa-la! This is my set up! I think overall it took me like 5 hours to finish building and setting up my desktop because I had to keep running to work to help my parents at the restaurant and coming back home when it wasn't busy anymore.
